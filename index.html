VARIANTS

Variants(VRS) are born from the same code base and each one is a result of experimentation. Within the collection there are siblings, relatives and colonies of a Variant and each of them are unique and are born out of necessity. Colony traits are categorized by the usage of lines, circles and motion. The DNA is the identifier for that Variant and the usage of prime numbers is to show off their uniqueness.

The first generation has 25 VRS and they all are green. The total supply will be 100 and given the volatile nature of Variants, evolution and mutatations are bound to happen and traits may be added or removed but the uniqueness will remain...
